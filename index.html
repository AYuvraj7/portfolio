About Me

Building a Legacy
Worth Remembering

I am Yuvraj Singh Thakur — an engineer turned entrepreneur, driven by the belief that skills and knowledge can transform lives. Based in Madhya Pradesh, I wear multiple hats: President of an Educational Society, Advertising Agency Founder, and Sports Academy Builder.

My journey began with Electronics & Communication Engineering from RGPV, followed by years in logistics operations at Amazon, Flipkart, and Ecom Express. In 2023, I chose purpose over a paycheck — and built Thrive from the ground up.

I am also Stanford Certified in Food & Health, bringing a holistic approach to community development that encompasses education, nutrition, and physical wellbeing.
