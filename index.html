About Me

I'm Yuvraj Singh Thakur — an engineer, entrepreneur, and community builder from Madhya Pradesh. With a background in Electronics & Communication Engineering from RGPV and Stanford certification in Food & Health, I sit at a unique intersection of technology, health, and social impact.

Over 8+ years, I've led logistics operations at Amazon, Flipkart, and Ecom Express, managing station-level teams and daily loads of 2,500+ shipments. Today, I channel that operational expertise into building institutions that create lasting impact.

My mission: empower youth through skill-based education, promote public health awareness, and build communities that thrive through discipline and excellence.
